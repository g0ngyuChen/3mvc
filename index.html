3Mvc Demo

Here is the demo page of 3Mvc,which is a multi-component VC model using multi-augmentation and multi-task disentanglement.

  • 3Mvc-a: using pitch averaging.
  • 3Mvc-p: using random pitch perturbation.
  • Baseline: SRDvc model.(https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.08757)
  • w/o Two Stage: 3Mvc-a without two stage training setting.
  • w/o c-recon: 3Mvc-a without content reconstruction.
